Frequently asked

  • Do I need an account? No. WorkTracker has no sign-up and no login — all your data stays on your device.
  • Where is my data stored? Locally, in an on-device database. It is not uploaded to any server we operate. Uninstalling the app deletes it.
  • Why does it ask for location? Only if you enable Leave Reminders, to set your Home/Work locations and estimate your commute. See the privacy policy for details.
  • How do I set my work hours? Open Settings → Schedule to set start/end times, working days, and lunch break.
